Communication strategy
2020

How to position yourself in the market and educate at the same time

The startup Cuidas is a corporate health management company in SP. Its service provides a dedicated Health Team responsible to caring and managing the physical and mental health of the employees of a company, with same day assistance and personalized healthplan for the team. Its innovative proposal has several challenges, one of the biggest being the explaining how the health system is suppose to work to the unaccustomed public.

Together with the communication and design team, we renewed our comunicational approach in order to explain to the public that access to health is possible, viable and beneficial for both the manager and the employee.

After analyzing the results of analytics, public and stakeholder research, we decided to focus the conversation on HR managers and decision makers in small businesses. The new narrative brings calming images, colors and familiar shapes to illustrate powerful arguments for business return. With Google Analytics and Hotjar, we kept tracking of the public's interaction and kept on learning to improve.

Digital Health Record
2019

Tailored tool for specialized professionals

Along with the family doctors and nurses who support the Cuidas service, we have gradually developed a Digital Health Record for Primary Care.

Based on the conventional consultation protocol structure, the software has exclusive tools for the Cuidas health team, which aims to make the appointments between professional and patient a lot more intelligent in terms of data storage and more humane, considering psychological factors more completely and allowing the doctor or nurse to dedicate more time to the patient than to the consultation record.

Appointments App
2018

An application that connects professionals and patients

Appointment and consultation application with health professionals from Cuidas that aims at contact at three levels: online consultation (through video call with family and community doctor), face-to-face consultation (viable in the patient's own workspace or in external offices) and quick contact (consists of a direct communication channel with the nursing professional for advice and simple problem solving via chat).

The application considers the agenda of two professionals dedicated to the client company, but it also facilitates the user's access to assistance including other professionals of greater availability in appropriate cases.
